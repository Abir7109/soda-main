📰 News Workflow Animation — “LIVE INTEL NEWSROOM”
Core Concept
Make it feel like SODA has entered a futuristic global newsroom / intelligence command center.

When the user asks:

“What’s going on in Bangladesh?”
“What’s happening in the world?”
“Show me today’s top news.”

SODA opens a multi-window live news wall, fetches headlines, separates them into cards/windows, then spotlights each story one by one like a news anchor presenting a briefing.

Think:

CNN control room + Iron Man HUD + intelligence agency briefing wall.

Main Animation Idea
“News Feed Wall with SODA Spotlight Presenter”
The screen fills with many floating news windows. Each window represents one news story.

Then SODA says:

“I found the top updates. I’ll brief you one by one.”

Each story card is selected by a glowing reticle, pulled into the center, expanded, summarized, then sent to a “covered stories” timeline. The next card moves forward.
